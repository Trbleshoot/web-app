Whiskey

2 1/2 oz — bourbon is preferred

Sugar Cubes

2 — or 1/2 oz simple syrup instead

Mint

The leaves from 4-5 springs

Instructions

Muddle the mint leaves and sugar cubes (or syrup) together. Add bourbon. Fill with ice, stir until frosty, and garnish with a mint spring.
