Vodka

2 oz

HG Golden Roots Syrup

3/4 oz

Pineapple Juice

1 oz

Lime Juice

1/2 oz

Mint

For garnishing

Cucumber

For garnishing

Instructions

Combine all liquid ingredients in a shaker and whip shake. Pour into a collins glass with ice, garnish with mint and cucumber.
