Gin

2 oz

Dry Vermouth

1 oz

Orange Bitters

3-4 Dashes

Add ice

Add big pieces of ice into your stirring glass

Stir

Stir the glass for 40 seconds, or as long as it takes for the ice to chill the drink

Orange Twist

Spritz into the drink, rub around the glass and on the stem, then place on top of the glass

Instructions

Combine all liquid ingredients together in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into a lowball glass and garnish with a cherry.
